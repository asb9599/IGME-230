Care for a Stroll?

Have you ever taken a walk through the woods at night? It's quite the experience. With the cool night air and the moonlight shining through the trees, it can be absolutely breathtaking. However, some might find the experience slightly unnerving. After all, you never know what's ahead in the dark, and that feeling of unease can seem overwhelming at times. To illustrate this, I have created a game where you will simply walk from left to right across a scrolling forest at night. I cannot tell you what exactly you will find on this walk, but if you are like me and enjoy a good atmosphere shrouded in the unknown, I encourage you to give it a try. So how about it, are you feeling up for a midnight stroll?
